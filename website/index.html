How it works

Three steps. That's it.

Simple enough that any clinic owner gets it immediately. No technical knowledge needed — ever.

Step 01
💬

Patient messages you

Your patients message your existing WhatsApp number as usual. Nothing changes on their end. No new app to download.

Step 02
⚡

AI drafts the reply

eight.ai reads the message and writes a reply instantly — in the patient's language, using your clinic's info, in your tone.

Step 03
✅

You approve and it sends

You see the draft in seconds. Tap Approve. Done. The whole thing takes less than two seconds.

🛡️

Not ready to trust it fully? Start on Approval mode — every message gets reviewed by you before anything sends. Switch to Full Auto when you're ready. You're always in control.
